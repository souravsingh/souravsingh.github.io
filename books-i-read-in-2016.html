Books I read in 2016

After a long limbo, I start out my blog again with my list of books. Here is the list of the books that I have read so far in 2016 and some which I am currently reading-

Read -

  • The Catcher in the Rye by J.D. Salinger
  • For Whom the Bell Tolls by Ernest Hemingway
  • Blink: The power of thinking without thinking by Malcolm Gladwell
  • Lean In: Women, Work, and the Will to Lead by Sheryl Sandberg.
  • Genocidal Organ by Project Itoh
  • The Little Prince by Antoine de Saint-Exupéry
  • Gray Mountain by John Grisham
  • Love in the Time of Cholera by Gabriel Garcia Marquez
  • The Remains of the Day by Kazuo Ishiguro

Currently Reading -

  • The Intelligent Investor by Benjamin Graham
  • The Whistler by John Grisham
  • Norwegian Wood by Haruki Murakami
  • The Chemist by Stephenie Meyer

Hope to Read-

  • The IQ84 Triology by Haruki Murakami
  • The Year of the Intern by Robin Cook
